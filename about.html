Our Mission and Vision

We started Muve to alleviate the pain of the moving process for many individuals and businesses. Why do we need to wait days or even weeks for our to arrive when Muve can deliver your items within an hour, seven days a week. Our mission is to really revolutionize the moving industry and get rid of those long delivery windows, unreliable or shady moving companies, expensive/unnecessary additional fees such as travel cost and charging you for flights of stairs. We can all agree that the last mile delivery solutions out there are broken and we set-out to provide a service and platform that puts you back in control. So whether you're making a purchase online or need something moved, you can trust Muve will be there every step of the way with a smiling face to help.
